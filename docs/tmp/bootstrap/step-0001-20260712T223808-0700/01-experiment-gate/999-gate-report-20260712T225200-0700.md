## Result

relevant but do not already make the same claim:

- `sched_ext` supports the subsystem-extension analogy, but not filesystem
  path-view policy.
- BPF LSM and fanotify are security or permission mediation neighbors, not
  path-view object-selection boundaries.
- FUSE, FUSE passthrough, and ExtFUSE are the required RQ2 comparison family.
- Bento, Wrapfs, custom filesystems, stackable filesystems, and metadata
  services are the required RQ3 boundary family.
- Agent/workspace and environment/cache systems provide source oracles and
  workload pressure; they are not final RQ evidence by themselves.
