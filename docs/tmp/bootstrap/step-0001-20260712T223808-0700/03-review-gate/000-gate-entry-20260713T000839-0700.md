## Fixed Story Under Review

`namei_ext` is a `sched_ext`-style VFS name-resolution extension point. It sits
between namespace/materialization and FUSE/custom filesystem ownership, with
eBPF LSM as a neighboring security hook rather than the central comparison.

The paper has three RQs:

- RQ1: expressiveness and sufficiency of a narrow VFS name-resolution
  extension.
- RQ2: cost compared with feature-equivalent FUSE policy implementation.
- RQ3: safety and boundary compared with custom or stackable filesystem
  ownership.

Table-only, static-table, and `table_redirect` counterfactuals are retired from
the main story.
